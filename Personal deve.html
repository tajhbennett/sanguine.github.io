12 Pillars of Life

About: A brief narrative about Michael, who met a millionaire. The millionaire told him about his journey to success and showed him how to apply the 12 principles to live a better life. These twelve principles serve as the foundation for success. This book by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener will motivate you to go above and beyond in your life, which will push and inspire you to grow into your best self.

Setting goals entails deciding where you want to go and then devising a strategy to get there. Setting goals that stretch you and force you to do things differently will lead you to the future you desire.
From this chapter it can be gathered that the author intends for the reader to set present goals and distant goals. I have dreams that I wish to accomplish which lead me to this instituation to gain a higher level of education to further strenghten my chances of achieving my goals.

The Proper Use of Time

We either bear the pain of discipline which weighs just ounces or we have to bear the pain of regret which weighs tons. Every day, your best opportunities are those that align with your overall goals and should be where you spend your time. Don't just finish the day; get from it. Spend your time on what is important rather than what is urgent.
This chapter enlightened me on the idea of prioritzing myself more, pouring more time into my goals and things that are relevant to me and having better management of time which would be a positive impact to my daily life.
